SpacEra

We are a group of brilliant people at SpacEra Inc. that are passionate about space and wish to spread civilisation throughout the galaxy. In the last few years, we have sent many people there with success, which then led us to gather highly important data about our solar system and the planets that reside there. As a result, we are able to deliver to you today the product of our research. Started in the 1970, we began as a small side project, that was then developed into a full scale business. As you may already know, our company specialize in sending our customers to space at an affordable price. We are also known to send numerous astronauts to collect important information about the solar system. Finally, we donate 30% of our profit to charity in order to help people back home too.

Simply click the Ticket tab up there, and our company will be able to send you to your preferred planet. To see data collected by our astronauts about the other celestrial bodies in our solar system, click the Planets tab. Finally, we offer a thorough timeline in the Information tab if you wish to learn more about the Solar System itself.
